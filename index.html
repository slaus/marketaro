Frequently asked questions

  • How does Marketaro measure the number of emails sent?
  • Each time you make a request to send one message to one recipient, it is counted by Marketaro as a message sent, regardless of the message being deferred or sent to the recipient's spam folder.
  • Do you offer tailored onboarding help or deliverability consulting?
  • Whether you are just getting started here at Marketaro or you have been a customer for years our Expert Services can help you make the most of your email program. We are here to make sure you are configuring your IPs correctly, take your deliverability to the next level, and so much more! Our Delivery Consultants are here to work with our customers to evaluate their entire email program and deliver in a detailed assessment to help identify areas that need to be optimized. We also have Onboarding Specialists to work with all of our customers to be sure that when you go through the setup process it is ensured that your email environment is built for success.
  • Do you offer assistance or discounts for early-stage startups or non-profit organizations?
  • Yes, we believe in helping businesses grow. Eligible startups can fill out the form at www.marketaro.com/startup in order to take advantage and enroll. It takes a matter of a couple minutes and valuable insight from our experienced mentors will be shared, as well as special pricing on Marketaro's email solutions. As for documented non-profit organizations (501c3) filling out the form at www.marketaro.com/register can receive up to 25% discount on Marketaro's email solutions after sign-up.
  • I'm ready to sign up. What do I do and how do I pay?
  • Great! Your next step is to select a plan that suits your needs based on your monthly email volume and amount of contacts you will be storing in the system. From there you will move on to creating your account. Marketaro accepts credit cards and will charge your card the first of each month. There is no contract! Each plan is going to be month-to-month giving you the freedom to add more email volume or contacts to your plan whenever you need.
  • What are the details of the free trial?
  • With the free 30 day trial you will be able to send up to 40,000 emails and store up to 2,000 contacts once your account has been verified for the free trial. To activate your free trial you will need to verify your email address. You may also be asked to set up two-factor authentication and/or create a domain whitelabel. If you wish to continue your free trial after the 30 day period you will be able to send 100 emails per day.
  • Why do I need to verify my account?
  • Account verification is required to help safeguard your account and to maintain deliverability for you and all of our customers here at Marketaro.
